Practical advice for a healthier, happier life

Healthy Eating Tips

Balanced Diet

  • Eat a rainbow of fruits and vegetables daily
  • Include lean proteins like fish and legumes
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Incorporate healthy fats from nuts and olive oil
  • Limit processed and sugary foods

Portion Control

  • Use smaller plates to control serving sizes
  • Fill half your plate with vegetables
  • Eat slowly and stop when 80% full
  • Pre-portion snacks instead of eating from bags
  • Be mindful of restaurant portion sizes

Daily Exercise Tips

Stay Active

  • Aim for at least 8,000 steps daily
  • Take the stairs instead of elevators
  • Park farther away to walk more
  • Do short exercise breaks during work
  • Try standing or walking meetings

Strength Training

  • Include strength exercises 2-3 times weekly
  • Start with bodyweight exercises
  • Focus on proper form over heavy weights
  • Work all major muscle groups
  • Allow recovery days between sessions

Mental Wellness Tips

Stress Management

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Try 10 minutes of daily meditation
  • Keep a gratitude journal
  • Learn to say no to reduce overload
  • Schedule regular "me time"

Social Connection

  • Maintain regular contact with loved ones
  • Join clubs or groups with similar interests
  • Limit social media for real connections
  • Volunteer in your community
  • Practice active listening

Sleep Improvement Tips

Sleep Routine

  • Maintain consistent sleep/wake times
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Keep bedroom cool (18-22Â°C) and dark
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Avoid caffeine after 2pm

Sleep Environment

  • Invest in a comfortable mattress
  • Use blackout curtains if needed
  • Keep electronics out of bedroom
  • Try white noise if easily disturbed
  • Reserve bed only for sleep and intimacy

Hydration Tips

Daily Hydration

  • Drink water first thing in the morning
  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Set hourly reminders to drink
  • Monitor urine color (pale yellow ideal)
  • Increase intake during exercise/heat

Healthy Alternatives

  • Infuse water with fruits/herbs for flavor
  • Choose herbal teas over sugary drinks
  • Eat water-rich foods (cucumbers, melons)
  • Limit alcohol and caffeine
  • Try warm lemon water in the morning
